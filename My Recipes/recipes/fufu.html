Fufu

Description

Fufu is a staple Ghanaian dish made from boiled starchy foods like cassava, plantains, or yam.Traditionally, it is prepared by pounding the boiled ingredients in a large wooden mortar with a pestle until smooth and stretchy.

Ingredients

  • Yam
  • Cassava
  • Plantain

Steps

  1. Peel and boil the cassava and plantains or yam until soft.
  2. Transfer the boiled items into a wooden mortar.
  3. Pound them together using a pestle, turning and kneading in between to ensure a smooth, elastic consistency.
  4. Continue pounding until the mixture becomes soft, stretchy, and lump-free.
