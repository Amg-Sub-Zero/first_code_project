Jollof

Description

Jollof is a popular West African dish that originated in Senegal. It is made with tomatoes, onions, and a variety of vegetables, and is typically served with rice or couscous.

Ingredients

  • Fresh Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Vegetables
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Protein of choice ( chicken, beef, or fish â€” precooked or fried)
  • Chicken or beef stock (or water if unavailable)
  • Tomato Paste

Steps

  1. Blend the tomatoes, onions, and peppers until smooth.
  2. In a large pot, heat the oil over medium heat.
  3. Add the blended tomatoes, onions, and peppers to the pot.
  4. Add the protein of choice to the pot.
