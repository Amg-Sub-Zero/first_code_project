Ampesi

Description

Ampesi is a popular Ghanaian dish made from boiled yam or plantain. It is typically served with a sauce made from tomatoes, onions, and peppers.

Ingredients

  • Yam or plantain
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Kontomire

Steps

  1. Boil the yam or plantain until tender.
  2. Add the tomatoes, onions, and kontomire to the pot.
  3. Add the protein of choice to the pot.
